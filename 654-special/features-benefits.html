100% Online Access

The membership is fully online and works on any device. You can progress at your own pace with permanent access through your personal Dojo U portal. There's even a dedicated app that you can use for iOS and Android.

It meets you where you are!

You don't have to travel anywhere to get a world class bagpiping education. We come to you - wherever you choose to sit down and turn on your device! No more gas money needed!

Global Community

An extensive, hundreds-strong, global community of pipers from all walks of life and levels of ability, who share advice, stories, and banter daily through our dedicated online community and live classes.

Live Classes

25+ live classes, running every week on every timezone, where you can get instant feedback from world experts, both to constructively critique your own playing and get support and advice on any topic you're curious about.

Interactive Courses

Whether your beginning the chanter, transitioning to the bagpipes, or you want to hone in on your rhythm skills - we offer several top notch, self-paced courses for you to work through as a Basic Member. Need help on any of the course material? The community is always there for you to help with a quick answer (you don't have to wait until your next bagpipe lesson to get help).

Thousands of Tunes in the Library

Access our endless bagpipe tune library - we have thousands! Enjoy PDFs, video instruction, reference recordings, and an ever expanding library of our new INTERACTIVE LESSONS. We have recorded and catalogued every class we've ever taught at the Dojo - it's been thousands. It's all at your fingertips as a member.

A Better Method

Sure, we have a lot of fun technology, but we also have a unique method of teaching (and a hunger for finding the best way to teach bagpipes). If you've failed in your efforts elsewhere, you simply must give us a try - we'll get you better, plain and simple.

Interactive Lessons

What If You Had a Private Instructor—On Demand? Have you ever wished you had your own private instructor—ready to sit with you, show you things again and again, at your perfect tempo, as many times as you needed? Now you do. Our brand-new Interactive Player gives you that same experience, anytime you need it. Hear the tune played perfectly, watch the sheet music light up in real time, slow it down, loop tricky bars, and replay as often as you want. Practice with the music—not just next to it. We’ve just implemented this feature, and we’re rolling out interactive versions of all our most valuable lessons and tunes—with more added every week. This is hands-down the fastest, most frustration-free way to learn piping and drumming we’ve ever offered.

Submit your playing for feedback, any time

Use the "recordings for feedback" channel in our community to upload audio or video of your playing for feedback, any time! Don't wait for your next private lesson - get one any time you want!

Tune of the Week

Optionally, dive in with the whole Dojo on our "Tune of the Week" - this is a new tune to look at, every single week. Sure, it's important to master your core repertoire, but it's also essential to explore new tunes, and expose yourself to new challenges. That's what the tune of the week is for. You can even (optionally) purchase a printed book of our tunes of the week for posterity!

Ask Dojo Anything!

Every Friday, Dojo founders Andrew and Carl host an "ask Dojo anything" session to answer your questions live! It's super fun, and a great way to wrap up the week. They'll answer anything!

Play the Tuning Game!

Basic Members get access to our amazing "Bagpipe Tuning Game" - it's basically a video game that helps teach you how to tune your pipes! Develop a great ear any time by simply signing on to Dojo U. Don't worry - you won't just be playing a video game all the time - we'll also have plenty of live classes to show you how to take your newly developed ear and apply that to getting your pipes sounding great!!!
