I don't believe in online lessons

Believe me, you're not the first person to have doubts about the effectiveness of online bagpipe lessons.

Our school is indeed a big shift from the typical “master-apprentice” approach to teaching bagpipes.

It’s true that some of the “in-person magic” might not seem as magical in an online setting.

The Thought Experiment

Imagine this: one beautiful day in Playa del Carmen, Mexico, I pondered the benefits of an online bagpipe school. What if we could bring the joy of bagpipe camp to everyone, year-round?

Here’s what I envisioned:

  • Accessible learning from the comfort of your home.
  • No more travel expenses or time away from loved ones.
  • A continuous journey of improvement, not limited to just one week per year.
  • A vibrant community of pipers learning and growing together over time.

And that became a reality with Dojo U. This system ensures you can learn at your own pace, with world-class instruction at your fingertips.

Advantages vs Disadvantages of Online Bagpipe Learning

Dojo University is perfect for bagpipers, no matter their situation:

  • Those without access to local instruction can start from scratch and progress to advanced levels.
  • Those with local instruction can enhance their learning and develop a well-rounded skill set.

The Pros List

  • World-class instruction from home
  • No commute or travel costs
  • A large community of like-minded pipers
  • Hundreds of learning opportunities weekly
  • An endless archive of resources

The Cons List

  • Not in the same physical space as your instructor
  • Some hands-on skills are more challenging online
  • Self-motivation is essential

I don't have the time or energy to practice

Totally understand that feeling — life is full! But here’s the honest truth: most people don’t actually lack time; they lack results that feel worth the time.

Think about it: People find time for Netflix, family, and other hobbies. But bagpiping often gets pushed aside because it hasn’t delivered a return.

Imagine if that changed. What if every minute you practiced led to real, tangible improvements?

*This time next month*, you could be sounding better, playing more confidently, and genuinely enjoying your music!

If those results felt real, would you find the time then?

Because that’s exactly what we’re offering: a system that finally makes it click. Once it does, you will want to make time for bagpiping.
