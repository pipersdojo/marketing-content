I don't believe in online lessons

Don’t believe in online bagpipe lessons? You’re not alone! Many have doubts about the effectiveness of this method. A transition from the typical “master-to-apprentice” approach might seem daunting. Indeed, there are challenges when it comes to teaching certain skills online. The “in-person magic” of a traditional lesson can sometimes feel less impactful over the internet.

The Thought Experiment

Picture this: a daydream sparked by the beauty of Playa del Carmen, Mexico. It was 2011, and while others were caught up in the moment, I was envisioning a revolutionary bagpipe education model.

I thought, “What if we could recreate the bagpipe camp experience online?” The barriers of cost, travel, and time away from family and work could be demolished!

Imagine experiencing vibrant bagpipe sessions year-round, with world-class instruction from the comfort of your own home, without the hassle of commutes or health risks.

Advantages vs. Disadvantages of Online Bagpipe Learning

  • Accessibility: Perfect for those without local instruction.
  • Supplement your learning: Enhance your in-person lessons with our extensive resources.
  • Community: Join a supportive network of fellow pipers.

Pros:

  • World-class instruction from the comfort of home.
  • Hundreds of weekly learning opportunities.
  • A vast archive of recorded classes and articles.

Cons:

  • No physical presence with your instructor.
  • Some skills require hands-on guidance (but we have solutions).

I don't have the time or energy to practice

Totally understand that feeling—life is so full. But here’s the honest truth: most people don’t truly lack time; they lack results that feel worth the time invested.

People make time for Netflix, family, and other hobbies, and that’s fantastic! However, bagpiping often gets sidelined when it fails to deliver a rewarding experience.

What if that changed?

Imagine a scenario where every minute of practice leads to tangible improvements. Imagine by this time next month, you sound better, play more confidently, and rediscover joy in your music!

If that sense of accomplishment was within reach, would you find the time?

That's exactly what we're offering with our system—it’s designed to make everything click, and when it does, you'll want to dive into practice!
