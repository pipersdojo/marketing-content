The Dojo methodology of bagpipe fingerwork is world renowned for helping struggling (or beginning) bagpipers to succeed. We're going to go back to absolute square one, and re-build your piping foundation starting with the correct building blocks this time.

The Dojo methodology for building a great bagpipe sound is the best in the world. It's systematic, starting from the most basic principles, building up to the techniques that can thrive in the world's top solo and band environments.
