How do I get there?

Whether you're coming by plane, train, or automobile, **Kansas City Marriott Country Club Plaza** is accessible and welcoming to guests from all over North America. Here's how you might reach it:

By Air:

Kansas City International Airport (MCI) serves the region, and it's the most likely landing spot if you're flying. Upon landing, take a taxi, Uber, Lyft, or rental car for the **approximate 35-minute drive** to the Marriott Country Club Plaza. Shuttle services may also be offered by hotels or third-party companies.

By Car:

Kansas City is conveniently accessible from **Interstate 70 (East/West)** and **Interstate 35 (North/South)**. If using a GPS, input the hotel's address: **4445 Main St, Kansas City, MO 64111**.

By Train:

Amtrak serves Kansas City with a station at **Union Station** (about **4 miles** from the Marriott). Taxis, Uber, or Lyft are available to take you to the hotel.

These are general guidelines, and your exact travel arrangements may vary based on your starting location and personal preference. Always check with the hotel for specific or updated directions.

What if I need to cancel? (Refund policy)

Cancellation Policy & Protecting Your Investment

*We understand that life happens — illnesses, emergencies, or travel issues can occur.* Because many of our expenses are locked in well in advance, we're unable to offer refunds for any reason.

However, you can still protect your investment — and it’s easier than you might think.

  • Consider Travel Insurance: Many attendees choose to purchase third-party travel insurance to cover their event registration, flights, and accommodations in case of unexpected issues.
  • How Much Does Insurance Cost? Plans typically cost about **5–10%** of your total trip cost.
  • What Insurance to Buy: We recommend using a travel insurance aggregator like **InsureMyTrip.com** to compare plans.

If you need to cancel, you file a claim directly with the insurance provider. *We’ll provide a short letter confirming your cancellation if necessary.* Having a travel insurance policy means you don’t lose your investment.
