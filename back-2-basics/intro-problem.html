Introducing the Back to Basics Workshop

Join us for an immersive three-day journey tailored specifically for individuals just like you—passionate, eager, and ready to enhance your bagpiping skills. Hosted in Kansas City by experienced instructors Andrew Douglas, Carl Donley, and Griffin Hall, this workshop is not just another lesson; it's a transformative experience designed to rebuild your foundational skills from the ground up.

Here, you will:

  • Master Core Fingerwork Techniques: Guided by expert instructors dedicated to ensuring that every participant gains personalized, actionable feedback on their technique.
  • Build a Solid Instrument Foundation: Learn the fine details of sound production to create a bagpipe sound that feels comfortable and resonates beautifully.
