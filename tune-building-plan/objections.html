I don't believe in online lessons

Don't Believe in Online Bagpipe Lessons? Believe me, you're not the first person to have doubts about the effectiveness of online bagpipe lessons.

Our school is indeed a big shift from the typical "master to apprentice" approach to teaching bagpipes.

It's indeed harder to teach certain skills over the internet, as we can't do much that's truly hands-on. It's true, some of the "in-person magic" might not seem as magical in an online setting.

The Thought Experiment

All of these challenging doubts crossed my mind one beautiful day in Playa del Carmen. It was 2011 — just my wife and I on our honeymoon, sipping cocktails, and daydreaming.

I recalled my love for bagpipe camp as a kid, and I realized:

If we could bring a bagpipe camp online, imagine the possibilities!

And so, over a decade ago, I took the leap: to build the world's first online bagpipe school.

Advantages vs Disadvantages of Online Bagpipe Learning

Dojo University is a fantastic product for bagpipers with and without access to in-person instruction:

  • World-class instruction from the comfort of home
  • No travel necessary, whether you're near or far
  • No gas money, battling bad weather, or health risks!
  • Hundreds of weekly learning opportunities at your fingertips
  • An endless archive of recorded classes, courses, articles, and more

Yes, ideally, the online experience isn’t exactly the same as being right there with your instructor. But with self-motivation and a little daily practice, you'll unlock your potential like never before!

I don't have the time or energy to practice

Totally understand that feeling — life is so full! But here’s the honest truth: most people don’t actually lack time. They lack results that feel worth the time.

People find time for Netflix, family, other hobbies — and that’s awesome. But bagpiping often gets pushed aside because it hasn’t been delivering a return on that time.

What if that changed?

What if every minute you practiced moved the needle in a real, tangible way? What if this time next month, you were:

  • Sounding better
  • Playing more confidently
  • Enjoying your music again

If the result felt real — would you find the time then? Because that’s exactly what we’re offering: a system that finally makes it click.
