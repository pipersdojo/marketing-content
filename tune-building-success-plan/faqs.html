I don't believe in online lessons

Believe me, you're not the first person to have doubts about the effectiveness of online bagpipe lessons. Our school represents a significant departure from the traditional "master --> apprentice" method of learning bagpipes, and while it might seem challenging, we are here to assure you of its effectiveness.

The Thought Experiment

Picture this: a bagpipe camp experience offered 52 weeks a year, making it accessible to everyone regardless of location.

  • Affordable education: No expensive travel costs and accommodations.
  • Health and safety: Eliminate commuting and associated health risks.
  • Support and engagement: Develop a community of pipers who grow together over time.

While in-person lessons have their charm, we’ve created a platform that provides world-class instruction right from your home, alongside a supportive community.

Pros of Dojo University:

  • Access to an extensive archive of classes and materials.
  • World-class instruction without the need for travel.
  • Engagement with a large community of like-minded pipers.

Potential Cons:

  • Online instruction may lack some hands-on guidance.
  • You must be self-motivated to practice regularly.

I don't have the time or energy to practice

Totally understand that feeling—life is so full! But here’s the honest truth: most people don’t actually lack time; they merely lack results that feel worthwhile.

We all make time for Netflix, family, and other hobbies because they deliver satisfaction. The struggle with bagpiping often comes from not seeing tangible progress, leading to frustration.

Imagine if every minute you practiced brought measurable improvement. What if in just a month, you found yourself sounding better, playing with confidence, and truly enjoying your music again?

If achieving real results became a reality, would you find the time to practice? That's exactly what our system offers—a way to make it all click!
