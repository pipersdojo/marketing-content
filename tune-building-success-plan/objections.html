I don't believe in online lessons

**Believe me, you're not the first person to have doubts** about the effectiveness of online bagpipe lessons.

Our school is indeed a big shift from the typical **"master-->apprentice"** approach to teaching bagpipes. While it's true that certain skills are harder to teach online due to the lack of hands-on interaction, there’s much more to it.

The Thought Experiment
Fancy joining me on a little journey? Picture this: a beautiful day in Playa del Carmen, sipping frozen cocktails, lost in thought about bagpiping...
I imagined a better way to learn—an **online bagpipe school** that brings together enthusiasts from around the world, allowing them to connect and learn, all from the comfort of their homes.
  • Experience bagpiping 52 weeks per year, instead of just one week at camp.
  • Cost-effective learning—much less than traditional camps.
  • Access to world-class instruction without the hassle of travel.
  • Integration into daily life, making bagpiping part of your routine, not just a fleeting hobby.
  • Building a community of pipers that grows and learns together over time.

**Despite the challenges**, we've proven time and again that the benefits of **Dojo University** far outweigh any drawbacks.

The Pros List

  • World-class instruction from the comfort of home.
  • No travel, gas money, or bad weather to contend with.
  • Join a community of like-minded, music-first bagpipers.
  • Hundreds of weekly learning opportunities, not just one!
  • Access to an endless archive of recorded classes and resources.

The Cons List

  • Not in the same physical space as your instructor.
  • Some skills that require hands-on help can be more challenging.
  • **You need to be self-motivated** and prepared to work steadily over time.

I don't have the time or energy to practice

**Totally understand that feeling** — life is so full. But here’s the honest truth: *most people don’t actually lack time; they lack results that feel worth the time.*

You find time for Netflix, family, and other hobbies, right? That’s awesome! But bagpiping often gets pushed aside because it hasn’t been delivering a return on that time.

But... what if that changed?
What if every minute you practiced moved the needle in a real, *tangible way*?
What if this time next month, you were sounding better, playing more confidently, and **enjoying your music again**?

If the result felt real — would you find the time then?

Because that’s exactly what we’re offering: a system that finally makes **everything click**. And when it clicks, people want to make the time.
