I don't believe in online lessons

Don't Believe in Online Bagpipe Lessons? You're not alone in having doubts about the effectiveness of online bagpipe instruction. It represents a **big shift** from the traditional "master-apprentice" model.

Yes, there are challenges such as:

  • Some skills are harder to teach without hands-on support.
  • The "in-person magic" of a personal lesson might seem out of reach online.

But let me take you back to a pivotal moment.

On my honeymoon in Playa del Carmen, I imagined the possibilities of an online bagpipe school. What if you could experience bagpiping instruction year-round, without the travel costs and constraints?

Here's what we can **actually offer**:

  • **52 weeks** of learning opportunities.
  • **Incredible sessions** with the world's best pipers, no matter where you are.
  • **Long-term strategies** that incorporate bagpiping into your daily life.
  • A **community of pipers** that grows together and supports each other.

Dojo University is a solution for bagpipers with and without access to in-person instruction. Many have transformed from beginners to advanced pipers through our program!

Advantages:

  • World-class instruction from the comfort of your home.
  • No travel, gas money, weather challenges, or health risks.
  • A large, music-first community of bagpipers.
  • Hundreds of weekly learning opportunities.
  • An endless archive of recorded classes and resources.

Cons:

  • You're not physically with your instructor.
  • Some techniques require more effort without hands-on help, but we overcome this.
  • You’ll need to be self-motivated and put in daily practice.

I don't have the time or energy to practice

Feeling like you lack the time or energy to practice? You're not alone. The truth is, many people don’t lack time; they lack results that feel worth it.

Consider this: people easily find time for Netflix and other hobbies — bagpiping just often gets sidelined because it hasn't provided a return on that investment.

What if that changed?

Imagine if every minute of practice led to visible improvement. In just a month, you could be:

  • **Sounding better**
  • **Playing more confidently**
  • **Enjoying your music** again

If the results felt real, would you be willing to find the time for it? That’s exactly the **transformation** we offer: a system designed to make everything click for you.
